Step 1: Upload a map

What's your operating system?

If you have a PC, your minecraft maps are in %APPDATA%\.minecraft

(Click start, type %appdata%\.minecraft, then select the top search result)


If you have a mac, your maps are in ~/Library/Application Support/minecraft/saves

(Open finder, hold down Shift+Command+G to open the Open Folder popup, and type in ~/Library/Application Support/minecraft/saves)


If you are running Linux, your maps are in ~/.minecraft
